SFTP: Cyberduck/FileZilla

The SSH key can similarly be used to connect with SSH-file-transfer-protocol (SFTP) software like FileZilla or Cyberduck.

caution

As a courtesy to other users, do not transfer many large files at the same time. Try only to transfer the data that you intend to use at any given time, and remember to remove the data and files you are finished working with. You are sharing the bandwith and disk space with your fellow colleagues and other scientists.
